The INFINITI M35: A Legacy of Precision and Presence

Introduced in 2006, the INFINITI M35 quickly made its mark as a dynamic mid-size sport sedan—seamlessly blending refined power, advanced technology, and bold design. It delivered a driving experience that was both confident and composed, appealing to those who valued performance wrapped in luxury. Production continued through 2010, after which it gave way to the M37, and later the celebrated INFINITI Q70. Though its time was brief, the M35 remains a favorite among enthusiasts who recognize timeless craftsmanship and precision engineering when they see it.
