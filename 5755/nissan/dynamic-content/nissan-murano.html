A Brief History of the Nissan Murano

The Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV that launched in 2003 as part of Nissan’s expanding lineup of utility vehicles. The Murano has offered a spacious interior with fold-flat rear seats, varying towing capacity, and a premium design. Over time, it has evolved through multiple generations, receiving updates in areas such as infotainment, and driver assistance systems.
