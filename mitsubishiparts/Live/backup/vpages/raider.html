A Brief History of the Mitsubishi Raider

Mitsubishi Raider was a mid-size pickup released to acclaim in 2006. Available in trims from LS and XLS to DuroCross and, in 2007, SE, it was known for its exceptional capability, for its fetching design, offered in both double- and extended-cab configurations, and for its roomy, relatively comfortable interior. The model was a favorite among enthusiasts during its short run, until that ended in 2009.
