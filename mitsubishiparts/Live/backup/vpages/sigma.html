A Brief History of the Mitsubishi Sigma

Mitsubishi Sigma was a hardtop sedan variant of the Galant, one that Mitsubishi offered for two model years during the line's fifth generation: 1989 and 1990. It was known for being a dependable performer, relatively comfortable, and eminently affordable, which made it a great go-anywhere city commuter and highway cruiser.

What are some popular upgrades for Mitsubishi Sigma?

For one, you can turbocharge or supercharge the 3.0-liter V6 engine, to make it more powerful. Secondly, you can swap out for alloy wheels and add a body kit with carbon-fiber accents for lighter weight and sleeker looks. Third, you can put lowering springs and performance shocks and struts in the suspension for better handling.

What is my Mitsubishi Sigma service schedule?

That depends on what your owner's manual says. Change the engine and cabin air filters, do tire rotations and oil changes, replace the oil filter, inspect the radiator, water pump, and the rest of the cooling system, and so on, as it calls for.
