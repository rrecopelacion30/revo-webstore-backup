A Brief History of the Mitsubishi Outlander

Outlander stands out in the compact segment as an SUV that makes a great all-around driver, whether your ride's a daily commute or you're on the off-road to a weekend getaway. Auto industry experts agree, too: US News & World Report, for instance, claims that the crossover handles well, that it's well-equipped tech-wise, and that there's decent room for people and cargo, while Car and Driver magazine adds that its interior is comfortable and Consumer Reports notes that it has a fairly responsive, composed ride and that the infotainment system is easy to use.

Kelley Blue Book's KBB.com, for another example, praises the availability of all-wheel drive (AWD), the stellar warranty, and highlights its stylish appearance. RepairPal adds to the accolades, giving the Outlander a Reliability Rating of 4.0 out of 5.0, ranking it 8th out of 26 compact SUVs. This shows not only how dependable the Outlander is but also how its ownership costs are lower than average, meaning fewer trips to a Mitsubishi service center and less likelihood of costly repairs.

FAQs About Mitsubishi Outlander Parts and Accessories

My stock Outlander is great, but can I customize it?

The Outlander is cost-effective, so customization is both accessible and affordable. The 2024 model, for instance, starts around $29,000. Adding a few options using Mitsubishi's Build & Price tool, you can customize a base ES trim with a 2.5-liter engine and front-wheel drive for just a few hundred dollars more, depending on the package, such as the All-Weather Essentials or Black Out Appearance packages.

My Outlander needs more power. How do I get it?

For a boost in power, consider modifications to improve the engine’s air-to-fuel ratio. Adding a turbocharger, cold air intake, and a performance air filter are straightforward upgrades for Outlander models like the 2024, which comes standard with a 2.5-liter, inline-four engine producing 181 horsepower and 181 pound-feet of torque. These modifications can add a few extra horses and provide a more capable ride.

What can I do with the cargo space in my Outlander?

With up to 64.3 cubic feet of space in recent models like the 2024, Outlander provides ample cargo capacity. Adding cargo accessories such as trays, nets, and roof rack crossbars helps you maximize this space and organize items for transport more efficiently.

I might want to resell my Outlander someday. How can I help it hold onto its value?

Protect your investment by outfitting your Outlander with accessories like all-weather floor mats, custom seat covers, and sunshades to prevent wear and tear. Regular maintenance and timely repairs using OEM parts also help retain value, ensuring your vehicle stays in top condition.
