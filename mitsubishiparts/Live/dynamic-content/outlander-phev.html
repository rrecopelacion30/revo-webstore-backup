A Brief History of the Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV

Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV is a plug-in hybrid SUV that's been popular among commuters, light off-roaders, and families since its 2018 inception. It's because, for one, of its quick, quiet ride -- one on the sportier and more powerful side of its sibling, the iconic Mitsubishi Outlander. However, it's also because of everything from great ground clearance to super all-electric range. That includes its upscale interior, too -- one with plenty of room for both people and cargo.

FAQs About Outlander PHEV Parts and Accessories

What are some popular Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV upgrades?

If you want to improve your SUV's looks, consider adding alloy wheels and a body kit. To mod it for performance, put in a turbo or trade up to a supercharger, and add both a cold air intake and a cat-back exhaust with a high-flow catalytic converter. If you want to modernize its tech, try adding electronics like custom infotainment and premium audio systems. For investment protection, put on custom seat covers, install sunshades, and drop in all-weather floor mats.

How many miles can a Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV get on a full charge?

The SUV has a combined range of 420 miles, but it can get up to 38 miles of electric range. You can keep yours a long-distance runabout by keeping it in the best shape you can -- by doing repairs when the maintenance schedule in your owner's manual says so, and by taking care of any problems it has sooner rather than later.

What is my Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV service schedule?

That depends on what your manual says. Usually, though, you rotate the tires, get an oil change, and replace the oil filter every 3 months or 3,750 miles, inspect the brakes (including the brake pads and brake rotors) and change the cabin air filter every 6 months or 7,500 miles, and so on.

What are some common Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV problems?

You might run into a defective battery, a faulty infotainment system, or suspension issues occasionally. If you need help with repairs, you're welcome to schedule an appointment at a Mitsubishi service center.
