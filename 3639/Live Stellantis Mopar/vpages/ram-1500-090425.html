Ram Truck 1500 is a full-size truck that's been around since 1981. It's been a light-duty offering under its own brand, however, since 2010, when Ram Truck separated from Dodge. The first generation took off in 2011 and ran until 2018. Cab options have ranged over the years from regular and quad to crew. Engine choices have included gas, Cummins diesel, and EcoDiesel. Trims over the years have run from Tradesman to Express, SLT, LaRam Truckie, Sport, R/T, and LaRam Truckie Longhorn. There have even been Lone Star and Big Horn models, the former for Texas and the latter for the rest of the US. In other words, the line has offered something for everyone: a no-frills work truck, a capable midrange, high-performance choices, and luxury options. Many are still available today.
