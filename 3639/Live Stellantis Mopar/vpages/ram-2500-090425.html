Ram Truck 2500 is a line of full-size, pickups that's been around since the 1980s. Its fourth generation, however, debuted in 2009. It took off in 2011 and ran through 2019. Trims over the years have included Tradesman at the base, Big Horn and LaRam Truckie at the midrange, and Limited at the top of the line. There's even been a Lone Star trim available for heavy-duty truck enthusiasts in Texas. Cab configurations have ranged from regular and quad to four-door crew and Mega Cab. There's also a diesel engine option available from Cummins.

Updates between the 2014 and 2017 model years included an optional air suspension, a new interior design, exterior style updates, and tech upgrades, such as premium audio. Plus, a new Off-Road Package was offered for all trims except Power Wagon. There'd even be two new variants available -- a Southfork Edition Package for the LaRam Truckie Longhorn trim and a Harvest Edition. The redesigned fifth generation came out in 2019 for the 2020 and 2021 model years.
