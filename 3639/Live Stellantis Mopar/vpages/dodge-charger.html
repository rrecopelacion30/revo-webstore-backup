Dodge Charger is a sports car that came out in 1966, based on an exciting 1964 show car. Over the years, the model would be available in body styles galore -- namely, as a subcompact hatchback, a full-size sedan, and a personal luxury coupe. Notable model years were just as many, and they ranged widely. Enthusiasts appreciated, for instance, 1966, 1968, and 1969 (including the Daytona and 500 models). 1970 would also be popular, as would the Shelby models of 1985, 1986, and 1987. A sixth generation was reintroduced in 2006, and it ran through 2010. 2019 would be sought-after also, as would recent trim variants GT, Scat Pack, and RT Hellcat. The seventh generation came out in 2011.
