Dodge Caravan was a minivan that came out in 1984, but its long-wheelbase version, Dodge Grand Caravan, came out 1987. The model's fourth generation began in 2001 and ran through a fifth that began in 2008. Trim options ranged from base SE, SE Plus, and Sport to SXT and ES. Notable model years were many between 2003, when the C/V model temporarily returned, and 2019, when a commemorative 35th anniversary trim became available.

They included 2004, when an Anniversary Edition package was released to mark the lineup's 20th year, 2005, when the popular Stow 'n Go seating system was released, and 2008, when Swivel 'n Go seating became available. After a mid-cycle refresh in 2011, which brought a few style and functional changes, 2012 brought a value trim, AVP, and 2014 brought a sleek Blacktop package. 2015 saw new features added to SE Plus and SXT Plus trims, and 2017 saw a few tech updates. The line of minivans was discontinued in 2020.
