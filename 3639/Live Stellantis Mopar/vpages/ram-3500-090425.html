Ram Truck 3500 is a full-size, heavy-duty truck that's been popular since it came out in 1993. Its fourth generation released in 2009, taking off in 2011 and running through 2019. It was available as a no-frills work truck, an expertly equipped midrange, and a premium model -- Tradesman, Big Horn and LaRam Truckie, and Limited, respectively. Cab options ranged from regular, quad, and four-door crew to chassis and mega. Single- and dual-rear-wheel configurations and a Cummins diesel engine were also options.

Notable model years included 2013 and 2014. 2013 Ram Truck 3500 models offered a High Output package for diesel models as well as a center axle disconnect with a BorgWarner transfer case for 4x4 models. 2014 Ram Truck 3500 made an air suspension available and featured a new interior design and some exterior style revisions. 2017 offered tech upgrades -- for example, a premium sound system, available for the LaRam Truckie trim. Two new editions also became available. One was Harvest, and the other was a Southfork package for the LaRam Truckie Longhorn trim. A redesigned fifth generation released in 2020, for the 2021 model year.
