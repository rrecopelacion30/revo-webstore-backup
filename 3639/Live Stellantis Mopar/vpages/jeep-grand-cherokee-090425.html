Jeep Grand Cherokee is a mid-size SUV that came out in 1993. It was designed in 1983 to succeed the Jeep Cherokee (XJ). The line's second generation (WJ) appeared in 1999 with its interior completely redesigned. In 2001, the model took off, with notable model years including 2004, when it got a facelift, and 2005, when, for the third generation (WK), Quadra-Drive II four-wheel drive became available.

In 2008, the model got a second facelift, and in 2009, it got a HEMI engine upgrade. The fourth generation (WK2) came out in 2011. Trims over the years included base, Laredo, Laredo X, Limited, and Overland. There was even a high-performance SRT8 model. Plus, you could get options like a height-adjustable Quadra-Lift air suspension, not to mention one of two other 4WD systems, Quadra-Trac I or Quadra-Trac II. A fifth generation (WL) was announced for 2021.
