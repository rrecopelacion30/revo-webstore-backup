FIAT® 500L is a high-roof compact minivan that came out in 2013. It was inspired by the original 1957 FIAT 500 and the current FIAT 500. The L in the model's name indicates a longer overall length.

The van featured a cab-forward architecture and reconfigurable interior called Cargo Magic Space, both conducive to passenger and cargo volume optimization. Trims over the years have ranged from Pop and Easy to Trekking and Lounge. It was even the first car to offer the option of an in-vehicle espresso machine.

Most notably, in 2018, the model got a facelift, alongside some interior revisions and tech updates. It was discontinued in 2020.
