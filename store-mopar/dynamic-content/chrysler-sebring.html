Chrysler Sebring was a mid-size car named in homage to the Florida city, raceway, and race of the same name. It came out in in 1995. There were three generations: 1995 to 2000, 2001 to 2006, and 2007 to 2010. Trims included Limited, Touring, and TSi. The model was refreshed and rebadged for the 2011 model year, becoming the Chrysler 200.
