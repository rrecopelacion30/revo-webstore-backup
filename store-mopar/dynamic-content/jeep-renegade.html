Jeep® Renegade (BU) is a subcompact SUV that was released in 2015. It came in base Sport, midrange Latitude, and luxury Limited trims, plus a ruggeder off-road trim, Trailhawk. The crossover also offered a selection between two 4WD systems, Active Drive I and Active Drive Low. They paired with the brand's Selec-Terrain terrain management system. 2019 brought a facelift and some tech upgrades before the latest model came out in 2020.
